Academic Programs

Explore our comprehensive range of undergraduate and graduate programs designed to prepare you for a successful career in technology.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

A comprehensive four-year program that provides a strong foundation in computer science theory, programming, and software development.

Program Highlights

  • Algorithm design and analysis
  • Data structures and databases
  • Software engineering principles
  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning
  • Computer networks and security
  • Mobile and web application development

Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer/Engineer
  • Systems Analyst
  • Database Administrator
  • AI/ML Engineer
  • Cybersecurity Specialist

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

A practical program focused on the application of technology to solve business problems and support organizational needs.

Program Highlights

  • Network administration and management
  • Web and mobile technologies
  • IT infrastructure and cloud services
  • Cybersecurity fundamentals
  • IT project management
  • DevOps and automation

Career Opportunities

  • IT Support Specialist
  • Network Administrator
  • Web Developer
  • Cloud Solutions Architect
  • IT Project Manager

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

A program that bridges business and technology, focusing on information systems design and management.

Program Highlights

  • Business process analysis
  • Enterprise systems and ERP
  • Data analytics and business intelligence
  • Information systems strategy
  • Digital transformation
  • E-commerce and digital marketing

Career Opportunities

  • Business Analyst
  • Systems Consultant
  • Data Analyst
  • IT Business Partner
  • Digital Transformation Manager

Master of Science in Computer Science

An advanced graduate program for professionals seeking to deepen their expertise in specialized areas of computer science.

Program Highlights

  • Advanced algorithms and complexity theory
  • Machine learning and deep learning
  • Computer vision and natural language processing
  • Distributed systems and cloud computing
  • Research methodology
  • Thesis or capstone project

Career Opportunities

  • Research Scientist
  • Senior Software Architect
  • Data Scientist
  • AI Research Engineer
  • University Professor

About the College

Established in 1995, the College of Computer Studies has been at the forefront of technology education for over two decades. We pride ourselves on our commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and student success.

Our History

From humble beginnings with just 50 students, we have grown into one of the leading computer science colleges in the region. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation and adaptation to the changing technological landscape.

Vision

To be recognized globally as a center of excellence in computer science education, research, and innovation, producing graduates who are leaders and innovators in technology.

Core Values

  • Excellence in Education
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Ethical Leadership
  • Collaboration and Teamwork
  • Lifelong Learning

Facilities

  • Modern computer laboratories
  • Research and innovation center
  • Digital library
  • Collaborative workspaces
  • High-speed internet infrastructure

Industry Partnerships

We collaborate with leading technology companies including Microsoft, Google, IBM, and Amazon to provide internships, workshops, and career opportunities for our students.

Accreditation

Our programs are accredited by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and recognized internationally for their quality and rigor.
